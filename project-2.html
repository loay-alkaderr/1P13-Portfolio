Project 2: Q-Arm w/ Surgical Container.

Project Overview

Project "Get A Grip" focuses on two primary objectives to enhance project efficiency and ensure the safe transportation of a container. The modeling team is entrusted with the initial main objective, which involves developing a lightweight and medical-grade 3D-modeled container. This requires careful consideration of factors such as weight optimization for efficient transportation and adherence to medical-grade standards to ensure cleanliness and sterility. Simultaneously, the computing team is tasked with the second main objective, creating a robust computer code for the Q-arm. This code is essential for executing multiple commands involved in transferring the container. The application plays a pivotal role in ensuring the precise execution of orders, facilitating a seamless transfer to the autoclave for sterilization without disruptions, and maintaining strict adherence to safety requirements throughout the entire process. Together, these teams contribute to the overall success of Project "Get A Grip" by combining design excellence with advanced computing capabilities to achieve optimal project outcomes.
